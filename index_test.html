Research

My research is focused on modeling the climate dynamics of the Eocene-Oligocene-Miocene transitions. I am using a range of ocean, climate and solid earth models to better understand the onset of the Antarctic Circumpolar Current and the initiation of the glaciation of Antarctica.

I compiled a global late Eocene topography as model boundary condition and I am happy to share it. It is available in different Southern Ocean gateway configurations and resolutions.
